why do you need a pet sitter?

whether you are leaving for a day at the office or zipping away on holiday, your pet gives you that same look...and it hurts you every time! but now you can stop feeling guilty! the boulder tail waggin', llc is there to give your pet attention, food and exercise when you can't. best of all, i come to you so that your pet can enjoy the comfort and safety of home even when you're away.

i've lived with all kinds of pets since i was a kid--cats, dogs, horses, hamsters, rabbits, fish and more-and have lots of experience caring for them, in sickness and in health.

please get in touch to discuss how i can be of service to you and your pet. i look forward to hearing from you soon!

-kari
