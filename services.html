services and prices

initial consultation (free!!!)
you, your pet and i will meet to get to know one another, as well as to discuss my services and the service contract.

30-minute dog visit ($20)
includes a walk in your neighborhood and one-on-one time
* purchase 10 walks in advance, and receive the 11th free!

1-1.5 hour dog hike on the trails ($38)
*purchase 5 hikes in advance, and receive 10% off

1-hour dog visit ($34)
includes a walk, feedings, watering, and, of course, loving. this service can also include bringing in the mail and the paper, watering plants, adjusting lights, etc.

30-minute cat visit ($20)
for up to two cats, this service includes feeding, watering, scooping, and of course, loving. this service can also include bringing in the mail and the paper, watering plants, adjusting lights, etc.

other services, other pets...
please contact us for a price quote for cat walking, pet photography, shuttle services, pet errands, and more.

An additional charge of $5 will be added to each visit made on the following holidays: New Year's Day, Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day, Thanksgiving and Christmas.
